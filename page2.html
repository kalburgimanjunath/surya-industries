About Us

Surya Industries is the leading manufacturers of Mild Steel Fastners like Hex Bolts,Tower Bolts,Carriage Bolts,Foundation Bolts,U Bolts,Eye Bolts,Studs,Tie Rods,Nuts & Washers Etc under one roof as per various national & international standards since 1999.The unit is located at Hubli(Karnataka) which is well connected by roads,train & air.We cater to the needs of Electric power transmission line,Automobiles,Thermal Power Plants, Mining Company, Petrochemicals & Various Other Sectors. The Organization is well equipped with full fledged in house facility for manufacturing & processing of Electric Plating, Hot Dip Gavanising,Testing & Inspection.
Surya Industries is approved by  HESCOM, BESCOM, CESCOM, GESCOM, MESCOM, Godrej & Boyce Mfg.Co.Ltd. L&T(ECC Division),BHEL - Ranipet, Major turn key electrical contractors, Major electric tower & Tele Communication Manufacturers.
